# You're Invited

If you've made it this far, you probably remember diagramming sentences — or you're a developer who just recognized a parse tree drawn sideways on a chalkboard.

Either way, you're exactly who this is for.

Sr. Barbara's Class is growing, and I'd love to build a small community around it: people who care about grammar, analytical thinking, and the pleasantly nerdy overlap between the two. Whether you're a teacher, a parent, a writer who wants to sharpen their instincts, or a programmer who sees the same patterns I do — there's a place here for you.

## What's Coming

The game is actively in development. On the horizon:

- More sentences at every difficulty level
- Expanded grammar support — pronouns, subordinate clauses, more complex structures
- Player discussions and community input on new content

I'll be opening up a space for players and developers to talk, suggest sentences, report oddities, and generally keep Sr. Barbara on her toes. More details coming soon.

## Come In

The GitHub repository is public — feel free to poke around, open issues, or just follow along. And if you want to be part of the conversation when the community space opens, watch the repo or check back here.

The more the merrier. Sr. Barbara would want it that way.
